How To Play Among Us

  1. Starting The Game: Join a lobby or create one.
  2. Gameplay: Whether you're a Crewmate or Impostor, avoiding suspicion is key. Use logic and observation to either uncover the Impostors or deceive the Crewmates.
  3. Crewmates win: By completing all tasks or ejecting all Impostors.
    Impostors win: By eliminating enough Crewmates so that the number of Impostors equals the number of remaining Crewmates.
